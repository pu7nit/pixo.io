Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, CompressYourPics is completely free to use with no limits or watermarks. We believe in providing quality tools without paywalls.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server - they stay completely private on your device.

Does compression reduce image quality?

Our smart algorithms carefully optimize images to minimize file size while preserving visual quality. For most images, you won't notice any difference!

What image formats are supported?

CompressYourPics supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. We're continuously adding support for more formats based on user feedback.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no artificial limit, but very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.
